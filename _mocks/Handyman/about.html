Our Story

From One Toolbox to Portland's Go-To Repair Team

Pro Fix Handyman started in 2005 when Mike Callahan, a licensed contractor with a decade of construction experience, saw a gap in the Portland market: reliable, honest handyman services that homeowners could actually trust.

What began as a one-man operation working out of a pickup truck has grown into a team of skilled professionals serving the entire Portland metro area. But our core values haven't changed: show up on time, do quality work, charge a fair price, and leave the place cleaner than we found it.

Today, Pro Fix handles over 800 projects a year — from simple faucet replacements to complete deck builds — and we're proud that 90% of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals.
