General Questions

We serve the entire Portland metro area including Portland, Beaverton, Tigard, Lake Oswego, Hillsboro, Gresham, Milwaukie, Oregon City, West Linn, Tualatin, Sherwood, and Clackamas. If you're not sure if we cover your area, just give us a call — we're happy to check.

Yes, absolutely. Pro Fix Handyman is fully licensed with the Oregon Construction Contractors Board (CCB #198547). We carry $2 million in liability insurance and full workers' compensation coverage for all team members. We're also members of the National Association of Home Builders.

We handle a wide range of home repair and improvement services including general repairs (drywall, doors, windows), plumbing fixtures, electrical work, carpentry, interior/exterior painting, and outdoor maintenance. Check our services page for a complete list.

Pro Fix Handyman was founded in 2005 — that's over 20 years of serving Portland-area homeowners. Our founder Mike Callahan had a decade of construction experience before starting the company, and our team collectively brings over 60 years of trade expertise.

Pricing & Estimates

Our standard rate is $85 per hour with a 2-hour minimum per service call. For larger projects, we provide fixed-price quotes based on an on-site assessment. Materials are billed separately at cost — no markup. We track time transparently and round to the nearest 15-minute increment.

Yes! For larger projects (typically anything expected to exceed 4 hours), we offer free on-site estimates. For smaller jobs, we can usually give you a reliable phone estimate based on your description and any photos you share. Request your free estimate here.

We accept cash, check, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Venmo, and Zelle. For qualifying projects over $1,000, we also offer 0% APR financing for 12 months through our financing partner. Payment is due upon completion of work.

Scheduling & Service

You can schedule by calling (503) 555-0142, emailing info@profixhandyman.com, or filling out our online quote form. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours and send you a reminder the day before.

Yes, we offer same-day service when our schedule allows — often 2–3 days per week we have same-day availability. For emergencies (burst pipes, electrical hazards), we offer 24/7 emergency service. Call us to check availability for today.

We schedule in 2-hour arrival windows: 7–9 AM, 9–11 AM, 11 AM–1 PM, 1–3 PM, and 3–5 PM. Your technician will call 30 minutes before arrival. We respect your time — if we're ever running late, we'll let you know immediately.

Warranty & Guarantees

Yes — all labor is covered by our 1-year workmanship warranty. If any repair we've done fails or doesn't meet our quality standard within 12 months, we'll come back and fix it at no charge. Materials are covered under the manufacturer's warranty.

We stand behind every job. If you're not completely satisfied with our work, tell us within 30 days and we'll either redo the work at no cost or refund your money. No arguments, no hassle. We've built our reputation on doing things right, and your satisfaction is what keeps us going.
