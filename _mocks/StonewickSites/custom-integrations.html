THE PROBLEM

Data Stuck in Silos

You've got a CRM. A booking system. An invoicing tool. Email marketing. Maybe a project management app. Each one does its job—but they don't talk to each other.

So you copy-paste customer info. Manually export reports. Email yourself reminders because your systems can't do it automatically. Every manual step is a chance for errors and wasted time.

Common Pain Points:

  • New leads sit in forms—never reach your CRM
  • Invoice data doesn't sync with accounting
  • Website updates require manual changes in multiple places
  • Appointment confirmations don't reach your calendar
THE SOLUTION

Systems That Work Together

I connect your tools so data flows automatically. A form submission creates a CRM contact, triggers a welcome email, and adds a task to your project board—without you touching anything.

After Integration:

  • Leads flow directly into your CRM
  • Invoices auto-sync with QuickBooks/Xero
  • Updates propagate across all systems
  • Bookings appear on your calendar instantly

APPROACH

How I Build Integrations

Not every integration needs custom code. Sometimes Zapier is enough. Sometimes you need something more robust. I'll recommend the right approach for your situation.

1
No-Code (Zapier/Make)

For simple automations between popular apps. Fast to set up, easy to modify, lower upfront cost.

2
Low-Code (Custom Workflows)

For complex logic that needs more control. Still uses platforms but with custom scripting.

3
Custom API Integration

For high-volume, mission-critical, or unique requirements. Most robust, fully tailored to your needs.
