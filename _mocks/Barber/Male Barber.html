Shop Policies

Appointment Policy

We operate on an appointment-only basis to ensure you receive our full attention. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but appointments take priority. Please book in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee or loss of booking privileges.

Payment

We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments. Gratuity is appreciated but never expected. Our standard service fees are listed above, with additional charges for specialized services discussed during consultation.

Late Arrival Policy

Please arrive 5 minutes before your scheduled appointment. If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your service to accommodate other clients. We value your time and ask that you value ours.

Health & Safety

Your safety is our priority. All tools are sanitized between clients, and our shop maintains the highest cleanliness standards. If you're feeling unwell, please reschedule your appointment at no charge.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our work. If you're not completely satisfied with your service, please let us know within 48 hours and we'll make it right. Your happiness is our success.
