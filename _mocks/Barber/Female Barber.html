Our Story

Founded in 2018, Luxe Hair Studio has become the premier destination for women's hair care and styling in the city. Our talented team of stylists combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create looks that are uniquely you.

We believe that beautiful hair is an expression of your personal style and confidence. From the moment you step into our welcoming studio, you'll experience personalized attention, expert consultation, and a relaxing environment designed to make you feel pampered.

Whether you're looking for a fresh new cut, a dramatic color change, keratin smoothing treatment, or glamorous styling for a special event, our team is dedicated to exceeding your expectations. We use only premium, salon-quality products and stay current with the latest techniques and trends to ensure you always leave looking and feeling your absolute best.

Salon Policies

Booking & Appointments

We recommend booking appointments in advance to secure your preferred time and stylist. Online booking is available 24/7. First-time guests are encouraged to arrive 10 minutes early to complete a brief consultation form.

Cancellation Policy

We kindly request 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. No-shows will be charged 50% of the service cost and may affect future booking privileges.

Payment & Gratuity

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments. A 20% gratuity for excellent service is customary but always optional. Prices listed are starting prices and may vary based on hair length and complexity.

Consultation

Every service begins with a personalized consultation. We encourage you to bring inspiration photos and discuss your hair goals, maintenance preferences, and lifestyle to ensure we create the perfect look for you.

Product & Aftercare

We use and retail only professional, cruelty-free products. Your stylist will recommend home care products to maintain your beautiful results. We stand behind the quality of our retail products with a satisfaction guarantee.

Satisfaction Guarantee

Your happiness is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your service, please contact us within 7 days and we'll schedule a complimentary adjustment with your stylist or salon manager.
