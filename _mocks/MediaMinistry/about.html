ABOUT THE MINISTRY

A Church Without Walls

Light Bearer Media Ministry exists to bring biblical teaching, spiritual encouragement, and authentic community to people wherever they are—no physical church required.

OUR MISSION

Making Disciples in the Digital Age

In an increasingly connected yet isolated world, we believe the Gospel can—and should—meet people wherever they are. Our mission is to produce high-quality biblical content that teaches, encourages, and equips believers for everyday faith.

Whether you're a night shift worker who can't attend Sunday services, a stay-at-home parent looking for spiritual input during naptime, or someone in a country without access to church, we're here to serve you.

"Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation."
— Mark 16:15

OUR VISION

Faith Without Borders

We envision a global community of believers who are growing in their faith, connected in authentic relationships, and equipped to share the Gospel in their own spheres of influence—all facilitated through digital media.

Our content is intentionally accessible: free on YouTube and podcast platforms, available in multiple formats, and designed for people at every stage of their faith journey. We believe barriers to spiritual growth should be as low as possible.

OUR STORY

From Frustrated to Faithful

Light Bearer Media started in 2019 in a spare bedroom with a $100 microphone and a passion for reaching people who felt disconnected from traditional church.

Pastor Marcus had spent 15 years in traditional ministry when he noticed a pattern: more and more people in his congregation worked irregular hours, traveled frequently, or struggled with social anxiety that made Sunday mornings difficult. They weren't leaving the faith—they were looking for faith that met them where they were.

"I kept hearing the same story," Marcus recalls. "People would say, 'I want to grow spiritually, but I can't make it to church.' And I realized—what if church could come to them?"

What started as a weekly podcast has grown into a multi-platform ministry reaching over 120 countries. Our Discord community has become a genuine church family for thousands who've never met in person but pray for each other, study Scripture together, and celebrate life's milestones.

OUR BELIEFS

What We Believe

Light Bearer Media stands on historic Christian orthodoxy

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God—the ultimate authority for faith and practice. All our teaching is grounded in Scripture.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit— coequal and coeternal.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, lived sinlessly, died for our sins, rose bodily, and will return.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. It cannot be earned but is freely given to all who believe.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, composed of all believers. We support local church involvement while serving the global body.

The Great Commission

We believe all believers are called to share the Gospel. Digital media is simply a new vehicle for the timeless mission.

OUR APPROACH

Why Online Ministry?

We're not trying to replace the local church—we're trying to reach people who, for various reasons, can't access one. We encourage all our community members to find a local church if possible.

But we also recognize that for millions of people—night shift workers, missionaries in remote areas, those with disabilities, people in countries where Christianity is persecuted—traditional church attendance isn't always an option.

For them, we want to be a bridge: a source of teaching, community, and encouragement until they can find (or return to) a local congregation. And for those who are simply seeking—exploring faith for the first time—we want to be a welcoming first step.
