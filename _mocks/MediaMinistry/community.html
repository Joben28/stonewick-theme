COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Our Community Values

1.
Be Kind & Respectful

Treat others as you'd want to be treated. Disagreement is okay; disrespect is not.

2.
Stay On Topic

Use the appropriate channels for different discussions. This keeps conversations focused and helpful.

3.
No Politics or Controversy

This is a space for spiritual growth, not political debate. Let's focus on what we share: Jesus.

4.
Protect Privacy

What's shared in prayer requests and small groups stays there. Don't share others' information outside the community.

5.
Report Concerns

If something violates these guidelines, reach out to a moderator. Our team is available 24/7.
