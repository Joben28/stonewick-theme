  • Email: Send to questions@lightbearermedia.org
  • Social Media: DM us on any platform

We select questions for our monthly Q&A episodes and try to address as many as possible.

No, Light Bearer Media is an online-only ministry. We don't have a physical building or in-person services. Our entire community exists digitally through YouTube, podcasts, Discord, and social media.

We encourage our community members to also connect with a local church in their area if possible, while using our content as a supplement to their spiritual growth.

Pastor Marcus is occasionally available for speaking engagements, conferences, and guest podcast appearances. Due to the demands of regular content creation, his availability is limited.

For speaking inquiries, please use the contact form above and select "Speaking Engagement" as the topic. Include details about your event, expected audience size, dates, and topic requests.

Absolutely! We encourage churches, small groups, and individuals to use our content for teaching and discussion. You may:

  • Show our videos in church or small group settings
  • Play podcast episodes for group discussion
  • Use clips in presentations (with attribution)

Please don't modify the content or remove our branding. For commercial use or redistribution, please contact us first.

We're always open to partnerships that align with our mission. We partner with:

  • Christian publishers for book giveaways
  • Faith-based organizations for content collaborations
  • Companies that share our values for sponsorships

For partnership inquiries, please use the contact form and select "Partnership/Collaboration." Include information about your organization and how you envision working together.

Light Bearer Media is non-denominational. We focus on core Christian beliefs shared across Protestant traditions. Our teaching emphasizes:

  • The authority of Scripture
  • Salvation through faith in Christ alone
  • The importance of personal relationship with God
  • Practical faith application

We intentionally avoid divisive denominational controversies and focus on what unites believers. See our Statement of Faith for more details.
