ABOUT THE MINISTRY

A Church Without Walls

Light Bearer Media Ministry was founded with a simple vision: bring authentic biblical teaching to people wherever they are—during their commute, at the gym, or in their living room.

Led by Pastor Marcus Chen and his wife Rachel, we're a community of believers from over 120 countries who gather online to learn, grow, and support each other in our faith journeys. No building. No dress code. Just genuine faith and real community.
