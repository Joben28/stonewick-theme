Belonging

Become a Member

Membership at GracePoint isn't about rules or requirements â€” it's about belonging. When you become a member, you're saying "this is my church family" and we're committing to journey with you.

What Membership Means
  • Commitment to Community

    You're choosing to invest in and be invested in by your church family.

  • Opportunity to Serve

    Members can serve in leadership roles and help shape our ministries.

  • Voice & Vote

    Participate in member meetings and have a say in key church decisions.

  • Pastoral Care

    Priority access to pastoral counseling, hospital visits, and spiritual support.

How to Become a Member

1
Attend Starting Point

A 2-hour class introducing our beliefs, mission, and vision.

2
Meet with a Pastor

A casual conversation to get to know you and answer questions.

3
Sign the Membership Covenant

A mutual commitment between you and the church.

4
Be Welcomed!

We'll introduce you to the church family during a Sunday service.
