Pastoral Care & Support

Beyond prayer, we're here to walk with you through life's challenges. Our pastoral team and care ministries are ready to support you in practical ways.

  • Hospital & Home Visits

    Our pastors are available to visit you during hospitalization or recovery at home.

  • Counseling Referrals

    We can connect you with trusted Christian counselors in our community.

  • Grief Support

    Join our GriefShare group or meet one-on-one with a care team member.

  • Benevolence Assistance

    Practical help for those facing financial hardship — food, utilities, and more.
