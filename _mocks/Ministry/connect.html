Getting Here

From Downtown: Head west on Main Street for 2 miles, turn right onto Grace Avenue. Church will be on your left.

From Highway 101: Take Exit 42 (Grace Avenue), head east for 1 mile. Church will be on your right.

Parking: Free parking available in our main lot and overflow lot across the street.
