Serving isn't just about filling a need — it's about becoming who God created you to be. When you serve, you connect with others, discover your gifts, and experience the joy of making a difference.

Serve Opportunities

Find a team that matches your interests and gifts

First Impressions

Welcome guests, serve as greeters, help with parking, and create a warm environment for everyone who walks through our doors.

Greeters Ushers Info Desk

Commitment: Sunday mornings, 1 hour before & during service

Kids Ministry

Teach, lead activities, or provide care for children from nursery through 5th grade. Help kids know Jesus in age-appropriate ways.

Teachers Helpers Check-In

Commitment: Usually every other week during service

Youth Ministry

Mentor and guide middle and high school students. Lead small groups, help with events, or provide supervision.

Small Group Leaders Event Helpers

Commitment: Wednesday nights + occasional events

Worship & Production

Use your musical or technical abilities to create an atmosphere of worship. Join the band, run sound, manage lights, or handle cameras.

Vocalists Musicians Tech Team

Commitment: Thursday rehearsals + Sunday services

Hospitality

Create welcoming spaces through coffee service, meal preparation for events, and fellowship coordination.

Cafe Events Setup/Cleanup

Commitment: Varies by role

Care Team

Support church members through prayer, visits, meals during hardship, and other practical help during life's challenges.

Prayer Team Meal Train Visitation

Commitment: As needed basis

Outreach & Missions

Serve our local community and beyond through food pantry, community service projects, and mission trips.

Food Pantry Service Days Missions

Commitment: Monthly opportunities + trips

Communications

Help share our story through social media, photography, video production, graphic design, and writing.

Social Media Photography Design

Commitment: Flexible, project-based

Facilities

Keep our campus beautiful and functional through maintenance, landscaping, cleaning, and setup/teardown.

Maintenance Grounds Setup

Commitment: Saturday mornings monthly
