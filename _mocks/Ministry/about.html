Est. 1998

How We Started

GracePoint Community Church began in 1998 when Pastor David Mitchell and his wife Sarah gathered with 12 families in a living room with a simple vision: to create a church where people could experience authentic community and encounter the transforming love of God.

What started as a small home gathering has grown into a vibrant community of over 1,500 members. But our heart remains the same—helping people belong, believe, and become who God created them to be.

Through seasons of growth, challenges, and change, we've remained committed to our founding principles: biblical teaching, genuine relationships, and outward service.

Our Mission

To help people find and follow Jesus by creating environments where they can belong, believe, and become.

Our Vision

A community where everyone has a place, faith is deepened, lives are transformed, and God's love overflows into our city and world.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our ultimate guide for faith and life.

Authentic Community

We pursue genuine relationships where people can be known, loved, and supported.

Grace-Centered

Outward Focused

Statement of Faith

What We Believe

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God. It is the supreme source of truth for Christian beliefs and living. We seek to understand and apply its teachings in all areas of life.

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. God is the Creator and Sustainer of all things, infinitely perfect and worthy of all worship.

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully human, conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary. He lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, and ascended to heaven where He intercedes for us.

We believe salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone. It cannot be earned through good works but is freely given by grace to all who believe.

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, made up of all believers. The local church exists to worship God, equip believers, and share the Gospel with the world through authentic community and outward service.

Pastor David Mitchell

Lead Pastor & Founder

Plan Your Visit

We want your first visit to feel comfortable and welcoming. Here's what to expect:

When We Meet

Sunday services at 9:00 AM (Traditional) and 11:00 AM (Contemporary). Services last about 75 minutes.

What to Wear

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans to business casual. We care about you, not your outfit.

For Your Kids

GracePoint Kids offers safe, age-appropriate programs during all services. Check in begins 15 minutes before service.

Grab Some Coffee

Our café is open before and after services. It's a great place to connect and meet people.
