Drywall Repair

Our certified drywall repair specialists handle everything from minor nail holes and drywall anchors to major wall damage from water leaks, furniture impacts, or structural issues. We specialize in seamless drywall patching with expert texture matching including orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, and smooth finishes. Each repair includes proper preparation, joint compound application, professional sanding, priming, and paint matching to ensure invisible results that blend perfectly with your existing walls.

Hole & Crack Repair

Fix holes from nails, screws, or accidental damage. Repair cracks and seams.

Water Damage Repair

Replace water-damaged drywall and restore structural integrity.

Texture Matching

Match existing wall textures including orange peel, knockdown, and smooth finishes.

Prime & Paint

Complete finishing with primer and paint matching for invisible repairs.

Why Choose Pro Fix for Drywall Repair? We use professional-grade joint compound, primer, and paint matching technology to ensure completely invisible drywall repairs. Every wall patch is properly prepared, filled, sanded to perfection, sealed with quality primer, and finished with precisely matched paint. Our licensed drywall repair contractors guarantee seamless results. Most drywall repairs completed same-day with no mess left behind.

Services include board replacement, post reinforcement, structural support repairs, gate adjustments, hardware upgrades, and protective staining and sealing to extend the life of your deck or fence.

We work on all door types including entry doors, patio doors, sliding doors, French doors, and screen doors. Usage

<!-- Light background -->
<section class="py-section bg-light texture-linen">...</section>

<!-- Dark background — add texture-ink-light -->
<section class="py-section bg-trust texture-grit texture-ink-light">...</section>

<!-- Intensity modifiers (faint / soft / default / strong / hard) -->
<section class="py-section bg-light texture-linen texture-faint">...</section>
<section class="py-section bg-light texture-linen texture-soft">...</section>
<section class="py-section bg-light texture-linen texture-strong">...</section>
<section class="py-section bg-trust texture-microgrid texture-ink-light texture-hard">...</section>

<!-- Arbitrary strength via CSS property -->
<section class="py-section bg-warning texture-microgrid" style="--texture-ink: 100, 50, 0; --texture-opacity: 0.07">...</section>
