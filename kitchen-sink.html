Component Background Rules

Match components to their intended backgrounds.

Light Background Components

Use on .bg-light, white, or light sections.

.card-service .card-feature .card-benefit .commerce-card .badge-gradient .metric-item .pricing-table-light

Dark Background Components

Use on .bg-dark, .bg-trust, or dark sections.

.card-glass .card-service-dark .card-feature-dark .commerce-card-dark .icon-badge .glass-badge .pricing-table
